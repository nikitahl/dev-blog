1. Information I Don't Collect

I want to make it clear that I do not collect any personal information from my visitors. I don't use cookies, forms, or any other means to gather data about you.

2. Third-Party Embeds and Ads

While you enjoy my content, please be aware that I use third-party embeds and ads from services such as YouTube, Vimeo, CodePen, CanIUse Embed, and Carbon ads. These services may collect data on their own, and their privacy practices are governed by their respective privacy policies. I encourage you to review their policies to understand how they handle your information:

  • YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Vimeo Privacy Policy
  • CodePen Privacy Policy
  • CanIUse Embed Privacy Policy
  • Carbon Ads Privacy Policy

3. Your Controls and Choices

As I don't collect personal information, there are no options or controls needed from your side regarding your data on my site. You can enjoy my content without worrying about your privacy.

4. Changes to this Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in my practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The date at the top will indicate when it was last revised.

5. Protecting Your Data While Visiting Websites

Even though I don't collect personal information, it's essential to protect your data while browsing the internet. Here are some general tips:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each of your online accounts.
  • Be cautious about sharing personal information on websites and social media.
  • Regularly review and adjust your privacy settings on online platforms.
  • Use a secure and updated web browser.
  • Consider using a virtual private network (VPN) for an added layer of security.
